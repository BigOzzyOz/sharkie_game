Manual

Movement
Use the keys for movement.

Fin slap
Use for a fin slap. Only stunned pufferfish are effected from a fin slap.

Bubble attack
Use key for a bubble attack. Stuns pufferfish, kills jellyfish and damage the boss with poison. When you are out of poison, it still attack the boss with less damage.
